INSTRUCTION

  1. Scan the QR code on the mural.

  2. Tap on the ‘Start Creating’ button.

  3. You will be automatically directed to your Instagram account.

  4. Point your camera to the mural.

  5. Wait for the shapes to show up.

  6. Different forms of shapes and text will be displayed on the screen.

  7. Be creative as you can be! All the shapes and text could be drag, resize, and rotate.

  8. You can capture your mural and directly post it on Instagram. Share your experience!

HOW TO:

Drag

Tap once on the chosen object, if it’s selected the shape will slightly become bigger. When selected the object is already moveable.


Resize

Select the object and pinch the screen to resize it.


Rotate

Select the object, put two fingers above it, and start to rotate your fingers to the direction you like.
